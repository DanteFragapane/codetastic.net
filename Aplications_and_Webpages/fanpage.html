Bio

Panic! At The Disco is an American rock band from Las Vegas, Nevada, formed in 2004 by childhood friends Brendon Urie, Ryan Ross, Spencer Smith and Brent Wilson. They play numerous different genres, from pop rock, pop, baroque, alternative rock, and more.
